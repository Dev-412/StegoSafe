How to Encrypt

Follow these steps to hide your secrets:

  1. Select Carrier Image: Choose a PNG image. This is the "cover" image that will hide your secret file.
  2. Select Secret File: Upload the file you want to hide (text, zip, image, etc.).
  3. Encrypt: Click the button to generate the new image.

Important: File Size Limits

The carrier image must be significantly larger than the secret file. Steganography requires many pixels to hide data without noticeable distortion.
